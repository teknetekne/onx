ONx Ventures is an innovative startup ecosystem established under the ON Digital umbrella as a crucial step in Burgan Bank's digital transformation strategy. ONx Ventures is not merely an investment structure; it is a holistic entity that brings investment, collaboration, and intrapreneurship under one roof.

By combining its regional strength with a global vision, ONx Ventures enables fintech and insurtech startups in Turkey to expand into the MENA region and international markets.

As ONx Ventures, we offer a value creation model that goes beyond direct investment and grows through knowledge, network, and strategic collaboration.
