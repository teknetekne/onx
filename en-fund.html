ONx Fund

The ONx fund carries the bank’s vision into the future by investing in early-stage startups that accelerate digital transformation, especially financial technologies.

Our Investment Approach:

Seed and Series A

Seed investments and Series A level investments in startups whose products or services are in the development stage

Strategic alignment

A selection process focused on strategic alignment, scalability, and innovation

Corporate synergy

A goal of long-term collaboration and corporate synergy beyond financial return

Focus Areas

Fintech

As ONx Ventures, we focus on innovative fintech solutions that aim to digitally transform financial services. The sub-areas we focus on for investment and collaboration are:

Digital Payment Systems & Wallets

Technologies that simplify the user experience, such as mobile payment, e-wallet, instant money transfer, QR / contactless payment solutions.

Lending Tech & Credit Technologies

Modern credit models such as digital assessment, credit scoring, alternative data usage, and micro-credit solutions in individual or SME loans.

Robo-Advisory & Digital Asset Management

Investment advice, portfolio management, personalized financial planning, and digital investment platforms with artificial intelligence / algorithmic support.

Open Banking & API-Based Banking

Open banking solutions, banking and financial service integration through APIs, data portability, and financial ecosystem collaborations.

User-Centric Financial Services & UX-Driven Digital Banking

Customer experience, interface / user experience (UX), personalized financial products, accessibility, and comfort-oriented financial services.

Insurtech

We focus on startups that digitize the insurance sector and produce innovative, technology-oriented solutions. The sub-areas we target for collaboration opportunities and investment are:

Digital Insurance Platforms and Insurance Distribution Technologies

Online insurance platforms that improve customer experience and make access easier by digitizing traditional insurance sales & distribution channels.

Underwriting & Risk Assessment Technologies

Solutions that develop more accurate risk analysis, pricing, and underwriting processes by using big data, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and external data sources (for example IoT, environmental data, demographic data). This makes the risk management of insurance companies more accurate and scalable.

Alternative Insurance Models & Product Innovations

Flexible and innovative insurance solutions that go beyond traditional insurance models and can be shaped according to customer needs. This expands access to insurance and creates innovative risk/production models.

Data, Analytics & AI-Based Insurance Technologies

Solutions that perform customer segmentation, risk modeling, pricing, forecasting, and operational optimization in insurance processes with data analytics, artificial intelligence, and machine learning. In particular, the use of big data and AI provides transformation and competitive advantage in the insurance value chain.
