FOCUS AREAS

02

Fintech

As ONx Ventures, we focus on innovative FinTech solutions aimed at the digital transformation of financial services. Our prominent sub-areas are:

Digital Payment Systems & Wallets

Mobile payment, e-wallet, instant money transfer, and QR / contactless payment solutions that simplify the user experience.

Lending Tech & Credit Technologies

Modern credit models such as digital assessment, credit scoring, alternative data usage, and micro-credit solutions in individual or SME loans.

Robo-Advisory & Digital Asset Management

Investment advice with artificial intelligence / algorithmic support, portfolio management, personalized financial planning, and digital investment platforms.

Open Banking & API-Based Banking

Open banking solutions, integration of banking and financial services via API, data portability, and financial ecosystem collaborations.

User-Centric Financial Services & UX-Driven Digital Banking

Customer experience, interface / user experience (UX), personalized financial products, accessibility, and convenience-oriented financial services.

Insurtech

We invest in startups that digitize the insurance industry and produce innovative and technology-oriented solutions. Our targeted sub-segments are:

Digital Insurance Platforms and Insurance Distribution Technologies

Online insurance platforms that improve customer experience and ease access by digitizing traditional insurance sales & distribution channels.

Underwriting & Risk Assessment Technologies

Solutions developing more accurate risk analysis, pricing, and underwriting processes using big data, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and external data sources (e.g., IoT, environmental data, demographic data). This makes risk management for insurance companies more accurate and scalable.

Alternative Insurance Models & Product Innovations

Flexible and innovative insurance solutions that go beyond traditional insurance models and can be shaped according to customer needs. This expands insurance access and creates innovative risk/production models.

Data, Analytics & AI-Based Insurance Technologies

Solutions performing customer segmentation, risk modeling, pricing, forecasting, and operational optimization using data analytics, artificial intelligence, and machine learning in insurance processes. Especially the use of big data and AI brings transformation and competitive advantage in the insurance value chain.
