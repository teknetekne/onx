InnovaClub

Intrapreneurship Program

InnovaClub is an intrapreneurship program that enables Burgan Bank employees to bring their innovative ideas to life through a systematic venture development process. The program is led by the Employee Experience Training and Development Unit, and trainings and mentorship sessions are provided by the Entrepreneurship Ecosystem.
