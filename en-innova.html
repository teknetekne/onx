INNOVACLUB

01

InnovaClub is Burgan Bank's intrapreneurship platform, turning employee ideas into validated products and new business models.

Ideas move from screening and pitching into discovery, where teams validate customer-problem fit. Projects selected at Demo Day advance to the pilot phase to build a minimum viable product (MVP).

InnovaClub Stages

Phase 1

Ideation & Selection

01

Idea Application

Employees submit their solution ideas regarding customers, operations, processes, or technology. The goal is to make opportunity areas aligned with the bank's strategic priorities visible.

02

Pre-Screening

Ideas are evaluated on paper based on level of innovation, feasibility, strategic alignment, and value creation potential. Idea owners passing this stage participate in the Idea Pitch process to make their first presentations.

03

Idea Pitch

Participants present their ideas to the jury to earn the right to pass to the next phase of the program. This stage is also the first contact point where entrepreneurship, problem definition, and presentation skills are evaluated.

Phase 2

Design & Validation

04

Discovery Phase (4 Weeks)

Selected teams go through processes of customer problem validation, user research, solution hypothesis development, and rapid solution testing. At the end of this phase, a presentation is made to management on Demoday, and successful projects proceed to the MVP process.

05

Pilot Phase (12 Weeks)

Teams develop a limited-scope MVP in an intensive 12-week program. User feedback is collected. A draft financial model is prepared. At the end of the process, a presentation is made to management, and successful projects proceed to the efficiency phase.

06

Efficiency Phase

MVP process successful teams work to develop final product and complete all blocks of business model. PoC is made. Financial projection and investment needs determined. Product first customer is Burgan Bank.

Phase 3

Commercialization

07

Commercialization

Projects passing to this phase positioned under Burgan Teknoloji AS, first external customer acquisition for the startup targeted.

InnovaClub integrates employees' creativity with strategic goals, accelerates the bank's digital transformation, and gives the institution the capacity to create long-term value. The program offers an end-to-end innovation model that ensures ideas are not only generated but validated, MVP tests are conducted, and if successful, commercialized to join the ONx Ventures ecosystem.

InnovaClub’s Strategic Role

Strengthens internal innovation

Converts bank employees' knowledge into innovative solutions.

Facilitates integration with external startups

Matches employee ideas with technology companies in the external ecosystem via ONx Network.

Embeds entrepreneurial culture into the institution

Spreads trial-and-error, speed, agility, and customer-centric approach across the institution.

Generates new business models

Creates new value areas through both bank-integrated products and independent startups.
